# Mission: Maging Competent ICT Professional

## Why
Gusto kong magkaroon ng solid foundation sa ICT — mula computer fundamentals hanggang sa modern technologies — para makapag-apply sa trabaho (tech support, programming, networking, o cybersecurity) at maging competitive sa IT industry. Kailangan ko ng structured learning path na Taglish para mas madaling maintindihan.

## Success looks like
- Kaya mag-operate ng computer, mag-file management, at mag-internet nang safe
- Marunong gumamit ng productivity tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, email, cloud)
- May kaalaman sa computer hardware at kaya mag-troubleshoot ng basic PC problems
- Nauunawaan ang networking concepts (IP, DNS, OSI, Wi-Fi, basic commands)
- Marunong mag-program sa Python at mag-script sa Bash
- May web development skills (HTML, CSS, JS, backend, database)
- Alam ang cybersecurity fundamentals at best practices
- May exposure sa cloud computing, IoT, at AI/ML trends
- May professional online presence (LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio)
- Nakatapos ng capstone project na nag-iintegrate ng multiple skills

## Constraints
- Self-paced learning
- Taglish ang medium of instruction
- Free resources lang gagamitin
- Hands-on exercises and quizzes ang learning style

## Out of scope
- Advanced programming (data structures, algorithms, OOP design patterns)
- Enterprise-level network engineering (CCNA-level deep dive)
- Specialized cybersecurity certifications (CEH, CISSP)
- Advanced cloud architecture (AWS Solutions Architect)
- Mobile app development (iOS/Android native)
